## Introduction

Hello, I'm not dead. I'm alive. I'm a game engine developer now.

I'm currently working on a game engine that I'm going to call `Zahlen`. It's a 3D game engine integrating Vulkan and Jolt Physics. It has an asset pipeline for loading models and textures.

Moreover there are PBR materials, lighting, and a lot of other features. 

VBOs and IBOs are completely unbound. Primarily a deferred renderer, but there are also forward passes for transparent objects and stuff.

Has a functional scripting layer. Not sure what games I'm gonna make with it, but it's a good start. 

I already have assets, conveniently from animators who created `The Amazing Digital Circus` assets. Why that show specifically? I'm not sure. It's just there. And I like the show.
